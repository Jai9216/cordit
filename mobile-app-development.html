Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Why should you get a mobile app developed? Or why does anyone need a mobile application?

Today every other person on earth has a smartphone in their hand, and if you want your business to grow, a mobile application can be your best option. Over 40% of sales made on ecommerce stores are made through mobile apps as they are user-friendly, make the brand feel legit, and provide numerous functionalities. Not only for sales, but a mobile app can increase efficiency and make operations easy for businesses and their clients both.

2. Should you go with an android app or iOS app development?

In the present scenario, there are more Android devices than iOS, but both are growing in numbers rapidly. And when there is an advanced technology like a hybrid or cross-platform app development available, why settle for one? We would recommend you get an app that runs on multiple platforms. However, if you don't want that, you should choose the platform based upon the audience, features of the app, and similar factors.
